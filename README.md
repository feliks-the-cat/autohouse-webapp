autohouse-webapp
================

The system of accounting and registration of car sales in car dealer

+ selling cars
+ preparation of contracts
+ viewing Reports
+ accounting  history of customer sales

Used technologies:
+ Spring 3.0.7 (Core, MVC)
+ Hibernate 3.1.0
+ JBoss 5.1
+ UI: JSP, JSTL
+ MySQL 5.5
+ Ant 1.8.4
+ Eclipse Indigo
